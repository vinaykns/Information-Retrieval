<p><b>European Center of Sustainable Development ECSDEV</b> is an international, cross-disciplinary research center founded in Rome Italy on February 10, 2010.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The ECSDEV's main areas of research range from developmental economics to urban planning and more.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Publications">Publications</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Center_of_Sustainable_Development&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Publications">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>ECSDEV produces a variety of publications, among them is the <b>European Journal of Sustainable Development</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> a double blinded peer-reviewed <a href="/wiki/Open_access_journal" title="Open access journal">open access journal</a>. <b>EJSD</b> was established as the official journal of ECSDEV, to provide an international forum for debates among diverse disciplines, such as human development, environmental and energy economics, health education studies, and related fields.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="ICSD_Conference_Rome_Italy">ICSD Conference Rome Italy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Center_of_Sustainable_Development&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: ICSD Conference Rome Italy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>ECSDEV organizes the 2017 International Conference on Sustainable Development (ICSD) in Rome Italy.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The ICSD is inspired from the critical challenge of human, environmental, and economic sustainability concerning the present and future generations in a global-scale context.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>ECSDEV has opted for a model in which wider availability is favored by the Open Access Policy.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
