<p><b>Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hindi_language" class="mw-redirect" title="Hindi language">Hindi</a>: <span lang="hi" xml:lang="hi">भारतीय विज्ञान शिक्षा और अनुसंधान संस्थान, भोपाल</span> ; <b><a href="/wiki/IISER-B" class="mw-redirect" title="IISER-B">IISER-B</a></b>; also referred as IISER - Bhopal) is an autonomous research and education institute in <a href="/wiki/Bhauri" title="Bhauri">Bhauri</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bhopal_district" title="Bhopal district">Bhopal district</a>, Madhya Pradesh, India. It was established by the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Human_Resource_Development" title="Ministry of Human Resource Development">Ministry of Human Resource Development</a>, Government of India in 2008 in order to incorporate research in basic science at undergraduate and graduate level, with equal emphasis on higher education for research and education in science. It is an autonomous institution awarding its own degrees.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background"><b>Background</b></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Indian_Institutes_of_Science_Education_and_Research" title="Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research">Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research</a> (IISERs) were created in 2006 through a proclamation of <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Human_Resource_Development" title="Ministry of Human Resource Development">Ministry of Human Resource Development</a>, Government of <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>,under the category of institiutes of national importance, to promote quality education and research in basic sciences. Soon after the announcement, two of these institutes at <a href="/wiki/Pune" title="Pune">Pune</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kolkata" title="Kolkata">Kolkata</a>, respectively, were started in 2006. This was followed by an institute at <a href="/wiki/Mohali" title="Mohali">Mohali</a> (2007) and at <a href="/wiki/Bhopal" title="Bhopal">Bhopal</a> and <a href="/wiki/Trivandrum" class="mw-redirect" title="Trivandrum">Trivandrum</a> in 2008. Each <a href="/wiki/IISER" class="mw-redirect" title="IISER">IISER</a> is a degree granting autonomous institution with a prime focus to integrate <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a> <a href="/wiki/Education" title="Education">education</a> and <a href="/wiki/Research" title="Research">research</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Academics"><b>Academics</b></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Academics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>IISER Bhopal offer <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> (Dual Degree) programme, Integrated Ph.D. programme in <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mathemetics" class="mw-redirect" title="Mathemetics">Mathemetics</a> and <a href="/wiki/Physics" title="Physics">Physics</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ph.D." class="mw-redirect" title="Ph.D.">Ph.D.</a> programme in <a href="/wiki/Biological_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Biological Sciences">Biological Sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Earth_and_Environmental_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Earth and Environmental Sciences">Earth and Environmental Sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">Mathematics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Physics" title="Physics">Physics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chemical_Engineering" class="mw-redirect" title="Chemical Engineering">Chemical Engineering</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Electrical_Engineering_%26_Computer_Science&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science (page does not exist)">Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bachelor_of_Science_-_Master_of_Science_.28BS-MS.29">Bachelor of Science - Master of Science (BS-MS)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Bachelor of Science - Master of Science (BS-MS)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The five year <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> (Dual Degree) Programme is offered in <a href="/wiki/Biological_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Biological Sciences">Biological Sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">Mathematics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Physics" title="Physics">Physics</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Earth_and_Environmental_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Earth and Environmental Sciences">Earth and Environmental Sciences</a>. The institute will also be offering BS-MS (Dual Degree) programme in <a href="/wiki/Chemical_Engineering" class="mw-redirect" title="Chemical Engineering">Chemical Engineering</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Electrical_Engineering_%26_Computer_Science&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science (page does not exist)">Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science</a>. All <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> students are eligible for either <a href="/wiki/Kishore_Vaigyanik_Protsahan_Yojana" title="Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana">Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana</a> (KVPY) or Innovation in Science Pursuit for Inspired Research (<a href="/wiki/INSPIRE" class="mw-redirect" title="INSPIRE">INSPIRE</a>) scholarship which amounts to Rs. 5000/- pm.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> (Dual Degree) programme consists of mandatory, common courses (core courses) for all disciplines during the first two years and discipline dependent professional courses during the remaining three years. Core courses include topics from all the four science disciplines in addition to interdisciplinary courses in <a href="/wiki/Earth_and_environmental_sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Earth and environmental sciences">earth and environmental sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Computer_science" title="Computer science">computer science</a> and <a href="/wiki/Humanities" title="Humanities">humanities</a>. In the final year, <a href="/w/index.php?title=BS-MS&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="BS-MS (page does not exist)">BS-MS</a> students are required to undertake project work with a faculty supervisor, relevant to their major discipline.</p>
<p>In addition to majoring in one of the five (<a href="/wiki/Biological_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Biological Sciences">Biological Sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Earth_and_Environmental_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Earth and Environmental Sciences">Earth and Environmental Sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">Mathematics</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Physics" title="Physics">Physics</a>) science disciplines, <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> students can also minor in another disciplines.</p>
<p>The Institute encourages and rewards academic excellence exhibited by its students. To this end, Professor <a href="/wiki/C._N._R._Rao" title="C. N. R. Rao">C. N. R. Rao</a> Education Foundation Prize with a prize amount of Rs. 5000/- per semester is awarded to a student scoring the highest CPI during the first year (in both the first and second semesters) of the <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> (Dual Degree) programme.</p>
<p>In addition, the Institute awards the President's Gold Medal for the best academic performance in the graduating class in all disciplines of the <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> program. Proficiency Medals are the awarded for the best academic performance in the each discipline of the <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">BS</a>-<a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science" title="Master of Science">MS</a> program to the graduating class. A Director's Gold Medal is awarded for outstanding all-round achievement and leadership in the graduating class in all disciplines.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Integrated_Ph.D._program_.28I-Ph.D..29">Integrated Ph.D. program (I-Ph.D.)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Integrated Ph.D. program (I-Ph.D.)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Institute offers Integrated Ph.D. programme in Chemistry, Mathematics and Physics. All admitted students will receive a fellowship of Rs. 10,000/- pm in the initial two years subject to satisfactory performance in their course work. Subsequently the fellowship will be revised to comply with the existing MHRD norms.</p>
<p>Students must successfully complete course work, qualifying examination, seminars, and a dissertation. Besides the mandatory requirements, students are encourages to participate in several professional activities such as workshops, review meetings and conferences.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Doctoral_program_.28Ph.D..29">Doctoral program (Ph.D.)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Doctoral program (Ph.D.)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Admission to the doctoral program is after a Master's degree in science. Besides the students of the Integrated Master's program, postgraduate students with a Master's degree in science from other Universities/Institutes are also admitted to the doctoral program.</p>
<p>The Institute also offers Ph.D. programmes in all disciplines. All selected candidates not receiving external fellowship are awarded an Institute fellowship. Currently, the Institute offers Ph.D. programmes in the following disciplines: Biological Sciences, Chemistry, <a href="/wiki/Chemical_Engineering" class="mw-redirect" title="Chemical Engineering">Chemical Engineering</a>, Earth and Environmental Sciences, Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics. All doctoral students are also expected to participate in the undergraduate teaching programme of the Institute as a part of their training.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> The program involved course work, a qualifying examination, a state-of-the-art seminar, thesis work, open seminar and a thesis examination, leading to the award of a PhD degree.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Postdoctoral_Studies">Postdoctoral Studies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Postdoctoral Studies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Postdoctoral studies are possible in Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology and Earth Sciences.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Engineering_Sciences">Engineering Sciences</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Engineering Sciences">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>IISER Bhopal is the first IISER to establish Engineering Sciences. Presently, the Engineering Sciences has two independent departments: Department of Chemical Engineering and Department of Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science. The key idea is to impart a broad knowledge of basic sciences and specialized knowledge of one of the engineering disciplines. The Engineering Sciences is specifically interested in developing programs that will enhance the industry-academia interactions, attract support from national and international organizations to conduct research in frontier areas and have an inter-disciplinary learning and research ambience at IISER Bhopal.</p>
<p>Currently, both the engineering science departments offer full fledged Ph.D. programmes. The students seeking admission in Ph.D. programme in any one of the engineering disciplines should have a master’s degree after GATE qualification in engineering from any reputed institutes approved by AICTE. During the programme, the students undergo vigorous coursework in advanced engineering topics followed by a research project as their Ph.D. Thesis.</p>
<p>The institute will be offering five years undergraduate BS-MS (dual degree) programme in Chemical Engineering and Electrical Engineering &amp; Computer Science commencing from August 2017 - which will provide the students with a unique opportunity to understand and gain the knowledge to pursue an academic career in engineering sciences along with natural science. The eligibility criteria for admission in BS-MS (dual degree) programme will be the same as the existing criteria for IISER Bhopal. The curriculum of the BS-MS programme in engineering disciplines will be interdisciplinary in nature. This will allow the graduates from these programmes to pursue careers in the fields that lie at the interfaces between natural and applied sciences.</p>
<p>The Engineering Science Departments also offer 'minor' in Engineering Sciences to BS-MS students majoring in natural sciences. This programme is intended to BS-MS students after completion of their second year (i.e. 4th semester). In the first two years, students learn natural sciences (such as chemistry, mathematics, biology, physics, earth and environmental sciences) along with other basic applied science courses and clear the fundamental concepts. After the 4th semester, students are offered to take minor courses of their own choice. The final year students, along with the courses, can also undertake a project in any one of the engineering disciplines with a faculty member, which is evaluated by a committee.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Admissions"><b>Admissions</b></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Admissions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="BS-MS_.28Dual_Degree.29">BS-MS (Dual Degree)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: BS-MS (Dual Degree)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Students are admitted to the programme through the following channels<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (<a href="/wiki/KVPY" class="mw-redirect" title="KVPY">KVPY</a>)</b>: Basic Science Stream: Students qualifying in SA /SX /SB streams.</li>
<li><b>Joint Entrance Examination (Advanced)</b>: Students securing a place in the rank list of Joint Entrance Examination (Advanced) for admission to Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)</li>
<li>Joint <b>Aptitude Test</b>: Students, who on the basis of their aggregate scores in class XII, are eligible for <a href="/wiki/INSPIRE" class="mw-redirect" title="INSPIRE">INSPIRE</a> scholarship by the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Science_and_Technology_(India)" title="Department of Science and Technology (India)">Department of Science and Technology</a> (DST) can apply for the IISER Aptitude Test.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Integrated_Ph.D._.28I-Ph.D..29">Integrated Ph.D. (I-Ph.D.)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Integrated Ph.D. (I-Ph.D.)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Admission to the Integrated Ph.D. programme will be made once a year during May/June. Advertisement would be floated between March/April. Candidates must have a Bachelor's Degree. A written or oral exam will be conducted at the campus. A prospective candidate should have completed a graduate programme (B.Sc./B.Tech./B.E.) in a discipline relevant to his/her choice of Integrated Ph.D. programme. Candidates seeking admission in Chemistry should have a valid JAM score.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ph.D.">Ph.D.</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Ph.D.">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Applications are invited twice a year. A prospective candidate should have completed a postgraduate programme (M.S./M.Sc./M.Tech./MBBS) in a discipline relevant to his/her choice of Ph.D. programme along with qualifying a national entrance exam. Students should have a valid rank in the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) and/or have qualified the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR)/University Grants Commission (UGC)/National Eligibility Test – Junior Research Fellowship (NET-JRF), or other equivalent examinations. All selected candidates not receiving external fellowship will be awarded an Institute fellowship.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Student_life"><b>Student life</b></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Student life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Students_Activity_Council">Students Activity Council</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Students Activity Council">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Students Activity Council (SAC) is a union of the students for organisation of extracurricular activities in the Institute and to address their concerns. It is totally managed, organised and maintained by the students themselves. SAC contains 6 different Activity Councils:</p>
<ul>
<li>Computing and Networking Council</li>
<li>Career Development Council</li>
<li>Cultural Council.</li>
<li>Fine Arts and Literary Council.</li>
<li>Science Council.</li>
<li>Sports Council.</li>
</ul>
<p><br />
And a Representative Council. The Activity Councils manage the extracurricular affairs that come under their realm, while the Representatives Council works as the channel to address all the issues of the students related to academics, hostels and student life in general. The Students Senate is the overall governing body of SAC; all the decision-making regarding the students is done here. SAC Elections are conducted once a year to appoint the President, Vice-President, Treasurer of SAC and the Secretaries of the respective Councils. All the students except those in their first year are eligible to take part in these elections. Students are also appointed for various other responsibilities in SAC. SAC ensures smooth and continuous conduct of student activities in the Institute. It also aims at quick and proper resolution of the issues of the students. SAC also provides an opportunity to the students to be engaged in the extracurricular activities apart from their regular curriculum, and present and enhance their creativity, technical and managerial skills.</p>
<p>IISER Bhopal has hosted the 4th INTRA IISER SPORTS MEET (IISM 2015), 9 institutes participated in this event ( 6 IISERs, IISc, NISER, CBS Mumbai).</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Enthuzia">Enthuzia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Enthuzia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Enthuzia is the cultural festival of IISER Bhopal. It was started in 2010. Three editions were conducted till now and the third edition was in October 2014. Ishwar Venugopal is the current Secretary of the Cultural council of Student Activity Council, IISER Bhopal</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Singularity">Singularity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Indian_Institute_of_Science_Education_and_Research,_Bhopal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Singularity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Singularity is the annual science festival of IISER Bhopal. It was started as a science club by a group of Science enthusiasts in IISER Bhopal to promote scientific thinking and rationale. Many science activities, talks were organised by this club in their initial years. In 2014, a step ahead was taken to promote science not only in the institute but beyond, in colleges and schools of India by conducting a Science fest of the same name. It witnessed students of all ages, who got inspired and excited about pure science through workshops, talks by eminent scientists of India, competitions, and other science related events. Singularity'14 was inaugurated by then Governor of Chhattisgarh, <a href="/wiki/Shekhar_Dutt" title="Shekhar Dutt">Shekhar Dutt</a>. Singularity'15 was organised from April 3-5. Singularity'16, the third edition of annual science festival witnessed <a href="/wiki/K._Radhakrishnan" title="K. Radhakrishnan">K. Radhakrishnan</a> as Keynote Speaker and Chief Guest. Students from IIT's, IISER's and various other institutions participated in various competitions held from 3-5 September 2016.</p>
