<p><b>Renishaw plc</b> (<a href="/wiki/London_Stock_Exchange" title="London Stock Exchange">LSE</a>:&#160;<a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.londonstockexchange.com/exchange/searchengine/search.html?q=RSW">RSW</a>) is a British <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">engineering</a> company based in <a href="/wiki/Wotton-under-Edge" title="Wotton-under-Edge">Wotton-under-Edge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gloucestershire" title="Gloucestershire">Gloucestershire</a>. It is listed on the <a href="/wiki/London_Stock_Exchange" title="London Stock Exchange">London Stock Exchange</a> and is a constituent of the <a href="/wiki/FTSE_250_Index" title="FTSE 250 Index">FTSE 250 Index</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renishaw_plc&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Company was founded by Sir <a href="/wiki/David_McMurtry" title="David McMurtry">David McMurtry</a> and John Deer in 1973.<sup id="cite_ref-history_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-history-2">[2]</a></sup> McMurtry had needed to measure fuel pipes on a prototype jet <a href="/wiki/Engine" title="Engine">engine</a>: at the time, <a href="/wiki/Coordinate-measuring_machine" title="Coordinate-measuring machine">coordinate-measuring machine</a> sensors featured rigid styli, which required manual positioning on the surface and which yielded poor repeatability when measuring delicate components. To meet this need, McMurtry invented a touch-trigger probe device,<sup id="cite_ref-history_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-history-2">[2]</a></sup> which he then <a href="/wiki/Patent" title="Patent">patented</a>. The probe featured an elegant 'kinematic' location for a spring-loaded stylus, providing a highly repeatable seated position for the stylus combined with the compliance needed to measure such components.</p>
<p>Renishaw was first listed on the <a href="/wiki/London_Stock_Exchange" title="London Stock Exchange">London Stock Exchange</a> in November 1984.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2006 the Company bought 'itp', a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> manufacturer of precision styli.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In early 2009 the <a href="/wiki/Late_2000s_recession_in_Europe" class="mw-redirect" title="Late 2000s recession in Europe">global recession</a> reached Renishaw, resulting in a large proportion of the workforce being placed "at risk".<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Since 2009, Renishaw has seen a turnaround in business, with orders doubling within a year,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> and the company stating that it had "recovered strongly since the worldwide recession".<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2010 Renishaw bought a stake (and subsequently took complete control) of Measuring Devices Ltd, a company providing a range of services in the field of surveying equipment.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2011 Renishaw purchased the 400,000 sq ft <a href="/wiki/Robert_Bosch_GmbH" title="Robert Bosch GmbH">Bosch</a> plant in <a href="/wiki/Miskin" title="Miskin">Miskin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Operations">Operations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renishaw_plc&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Operations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Renishaw specialises in industrial <a href="/wiki/Metrology" title="Metrology">metrology</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spectroscopy" title="Spectroscopy">spectroscopy</a> and sells its products in the major markets around the world. The company's strategy has been to grow organically sustained by patented innovations. The company's product portfolio has since expanded into touch probes for <a href="/wiki/CNC" class="mw-redirect" title="CNC">CNC</a> machine tools, <a href="/wiki/Calibration" title="Calibration">calibration</a> systems that optimise the performance of CNC machinery, <a href="/wiki/Linear_encoder" title="Linear encoder">linear encoder</a> systems, <a href="/wiki/Rotary_encoder" title="Rotary encoder">rotary encoder</a> systems, <a href="/wiki/Additive_manufacturing" class="mw-redirect" title="Additive manufacturing">additive manufacturing</a> machines, dental <a href="/wiki/CAD/CAM" class="mw-redirect" title="CAD/CAM">CAD/CAM</a> systems, <a href="/wiki/Raman_spectroscopy" title="Raman spectroscopy">Raman spectroscopy</a> and medical devices for functional <a href="/wiki/Neurosurgery" title="Neurosurgery">neurosurgery</a> applications.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Locations">Locations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renishaw_plc&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Locations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Renishaw's main offices are situated in an old <a href="/wiki/Mill_(factory)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mill (factory)">mill</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Industrial_revolution" class="mw-redirect" title="Industrial revolution">industrial revolution</a>, with several new buildings on a 26-acre (110,000&#160;m<sup>2</sup>) site. It is a <a href="/wiki/Nature" title="Nature">nature</a> oriented site, with dozens of <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">birds</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Duck" title="Duck">ducks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peacock" class="mw-redirect" title="Peacock">peacocks</a>) and a large <a href="/wiki/Lake" title="Lake">lake</a>. The site is also close to the village of <a href="/wiki/Charfield" title="Charfield">Charfield</a>. The company has a machine shop located at <a href="/wiki/Stonehouse,_Gloucestershire" title="Stonehouse, Gloucestershire">Stonehouse, Gloucestershire</a>, and an assembly facility at <a href="/wiki/Woodchester" title="Woodchester">Woodchester</a>, both near <a href="/wiki/Stroud" title="Stroud">Stroud</a>. There are further assembly facilities in <a href="/wiki/Dublin" title="Dublin">Dublin</a> (<a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Pune" title="Pune">Pune</a> (<a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>). Renishaw also has research facilities located in <a href="/wiki/Wotton-under-Edge" title="Wotton-under-Edge">Wotton-under-Edge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ljubljana" title="Ljubljana">Ljubljana</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
