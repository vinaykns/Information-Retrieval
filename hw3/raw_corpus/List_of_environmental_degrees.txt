<p>This is a <b>list of environmental degrees</b>, including for such interdisciplinary fields as <a href="/wiki/Environmental_science" title="Environmental science">environmental science</a>, <a href="/wiki/Environmental_studies" title="Environmental studies">environmental studies</a>, <a href="/wiki/Environmental_engineering" title="Environmental engineering">environmental engineering</a>, <a href="/wiki/Environmental_planning" title="Environmental planning">environmental planning</a>, <a href="/wiki/Environmental_policy" title="Environmental policy">environmental policy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sustainability_science" title="Sustainability science">sustainability science</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sustainability_studies" title="Sustainability studies">studies</a>, etc., at both undergraduate and graduate levels.</p>
