<p><b>Autosport International</b> is a four-day event motor-show that includes a two day dedicated trade show for industry professionals and a two day dedicated public show for motorsport fans..<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p><br />
It is held at the <a href="/wiki/National_Exhibition_Centre" title="National Exhibition Centre">NEC</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Birmingham" title="Birmingham">Birmingham</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a> and operated by <a href="/wiki/Haymarket_Media_Group#Haymarket_Exhibitions" title="Haymarket Media Group">Autosport Media UK - part of Motorsport Network</a>, usually in the second week of January. The 2018 show will be held on 11th - 14th January.</p>
<p>Its vital statistics include 27,000 trade visitors and 60,000 public visitors in attendance with over 5,000 overseas visitors and an 80% male demographic.</p>
<p>All categories of track and off-road motor racing are represented, including <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a>, <a href="/wiki/British_Touring_Car_Championship" title="British Touring Car Championship">BTCC</a>, <a href="/wiki/24_Hours_of_Le_Mans" title="24 Hours of Le Mans">24 Hours of Le Mans</a> and much, much more. The show also has an adrenaline packed Live Action Arena where spectators can witness motorsport action and stunts up close and personal.</p>
<p>The show has close links with many industry magazine titles. These publications inlclude <i><a href="/wiki/Autosport" title="Autosport">Autosport</a></i>, <i>Motorsport News</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/F1_Racing" title="F1 Racing">F1 Racing</a></i>, who all actively participate at the show.</p>
<p>Industry bodies also have close affiliations entitling members to many discounts and offers. These include the <a href="/wiki/Motor_Sports_Association" title="Motor Sports Association">Motor Sports Association</a> and the <a href="/wiki/British_Racing_and_Sports_Car_Club" title="British Racing and Sports Car Club">British Racing and Sports Car Club</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
