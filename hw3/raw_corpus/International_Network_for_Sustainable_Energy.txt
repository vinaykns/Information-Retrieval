<p>The <b>International Network for Sustainable Energy</b> (<b>INFORSE</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/International" title="International">international</a>, <a href="/wiki/Non-profit_organisation" class="mw-redirect" title="Non-profit organisation">non-profit organisation</a> (NGO) network of <a href="/wiki/Environmental_organization" title="Environmental organization">environmental organizations</a> promoting <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_energy" title="Sustainable energy">sustainable energy</a> to protect <a href="/wiki/Environment_(biophysical)" title="Environment (biophysical)">the environment</a> and to decrease <a href="/wiki/Poverty" title="Poverty">poverty</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activities">Activities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=International_Network_for_Sustainable_Energy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Activities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Develops scenarios for a transition to a 100% renewables energy supply with factor four <a href="/wiki/Efficient_energy_use" title="Efficient energy use">energy efficiency</a> by 2050. A global, EU-27 and European national scenarios are developed. The name of the sustainable energy model is <i>INFORSE Vision 2050</i>.,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li>World-wide database of about 1000 contacts,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> web based resources on EU policy,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> school materials,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> successes<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li>Online course material (DIERET)<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></li>
<li>Quarterly newsletter: Sustainable Energy News (SEN) published since 1992<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></li>
<li>Participation on UN Conferences as NGO observer as INFORSE has <a href="/wiki/Consultative_Status" class="mw-redirect" title="Consultative Status">Consultative Status</a> at UN <a href="/wiki/ECOSOC" class="mw-redirect" title="ECOSOC">ECOSOC</a> and <a href="/wiki/UNFCCC" class="mw-redirect" title="UNFCCC">UNFCCC</a>. Participation at e.g.,:
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/UN" class="mw-redirect" title="UN">UN</a> <a href="/wiki/Commission_on_Sustainable_Development" title="Commission on Sustainable Development">Commission on Sustainable Development</a> (CSD) (CSD9, CSD14 and CSD15 in New York)<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/UNFCCC" class="mw-redirect" title="UNFCCC">UNFCCC</a> (e.g., Exhibition and side event at COP 14, Poznan, 2008;<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> and COP 15 Copenhagen<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></li>
<li>Beijing International Renewable Energy Conference (BIREC 2005), Beijing, China<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></li>
<li>Renewables'04, Bonn <a href="/wiki/International_Renewable_Energy_Conference" title="International Renewable Energy Conference">International Renewable Energy Conference</a>, Bonn 2004<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></li>
<li>WSSD, <a href="/wiki/World_Summit_on_Sustainable_Development" class="mw-redirect" title="World Summit on Sustainable Development">World Summit on Sustainable Development</a> (Earth Summit 2002) in Johannesburg in 2002.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Participation on <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">EU</a> Consultations, Press Releases.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></li>
<li>Organising European NGO Policy Seminars.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></li>
<li>Exhibition, participation at EU events, e.g., EU Green Week, Sustainable Energy Week. See more at <a href="/wiki/INFORSE-Europe" title="INFORSE-Europe">INFORSE-Europe</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=International_Network_for_Sustainable_Energy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>INFORSE was established in 1992 at the Global Forum, which was a parallel forum to the <a href="/wiki/UNCED" class="mw-redirect" title="UNCED">UNCED</a> Conference so called <a href="/wiki/Earth_Summit" title="Earth Summit">Earth Summit</a>. In 1998, INFORSE got consultative status to United Nation's <a href="/wiki/ECOSOC" class="mw-redirect" title="ECOSOC">ECOSOC</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> INFORSE is working through Regional Coordinators in Asia, Africa, Europe, Americas. The INFORSE Secretariat is in Denmark. In 2002, <a href="/wiki/INFORSE-Europe" title="INFORSE-Europe">INFORSE-Europe</a> was registered in Denmark as an NGO network working on European level.</p>
