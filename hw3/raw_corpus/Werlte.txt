<p><b>Werlte</b> is a town in the <a href="/wiki/Emsland" title="Emsland">Emsland</a> district, in <a href="/wiki/Lower_Saxony" title="Lower Saxony">Lower Saxony</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.</p>
<p>Werlte was a host city of the <a href="/wiki/2008_Team_Long_Track_World_Championship" title="2008 Team Long Track World Championship">2008 Team Long Track World Championship</a>. The championship was won by <a href="/wiki/Germany_national_long_track_team" title="Germany national long track team">Germany</a>.</p>
