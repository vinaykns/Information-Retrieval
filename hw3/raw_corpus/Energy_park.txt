			<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Energy park</h1>
									<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
									<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
								<div id="contentSub"></div>
												<div id="jump-to-nav" class="mw-jump">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
				</div>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p>An <b>energy park</b> is a separate area used and planned for the purpose of <a href="/wiki/Clean_energy" class="mw-redirect" title="Clean energy">clean energy</a> development, like <a href="/wiki/Wind_power" title="Wind power">wind</a> and <a href="/wiki/Solar_energy" title="Solar energy">solar</a> generation facilities.</p>
<p>Energy parks create many other economic development benefits too. In <a href="/wiki/Ohio" title="Ohio">Ohio</a>, energy parks are creating thousands of <a href="/wiki/Green_job" title="Green job">green jobs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In Minnesota, community wind parks are also popular.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In England, <a href="/wiki/Wind_park" class="mw-redirect" title="Wind park">wind parks</a> are commonly known as <a href="/wiki/Wind_farm" title="Wind farm">wind farms</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> A more "lightweight" version of an energy park is a wind park or <a href="/wiki/Solar_park" class="mw-redirect" title="Solar park">solar park</a>. These have one type of clean energy generation, rather than two or more technologies, as in an energy park.</p>
<p>Some energy parks feature additional features beyond clean energy generation. Additional benefits include: green job creation, <a href="/wiki/Smart_grid" title="Smart grid">Smart grid</a> connections, as well as new recreational, technology innovation and agricultural opportunities. The Stamford Energy Park in Vermont is one example of an integrated energy park.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
