<p><b>Mary Ann Liebert, Inc.</b> is a privately held independent <a href="/wiki/Publishing_company" class="mw-redirect" title="Publishing company">publishing company</a> founded by its <a href="/wiki/President" title="President">president</a>, Mary Ann Liebert, in 1980.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:1_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-3">[3]</a></sup> The company publishes <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Academic_journal" title="Academic journal">academic journals</a>, <a href="/wiki/Book" title="Book">books</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Trade_magazine" title="Trade magazine">trade magazines</a> in the areas of <a href="/wiki/Biotechnology" title="Biotechnology">biotechnology</a>, bio<a href="/wiki/Medical_research" title="Medical research">medical research</a>/<a href="/wiki/Life_sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Life sciences">life sciences</a>, clinical <a href="/wiki/Medicine" title="Medicine">medicine</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alternative_medicine" title="Alternative medicine">alternative/integrative medicine</a>, <a href="/wiki/Surgery" title="Surgery">surgery</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Law" title="Law">law</a>. Its headquarters are in <a href="/wiki/New_Rochelle,_New_York" title="New Rochelle, New York">New Rochelle, New York</a>, United States.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
