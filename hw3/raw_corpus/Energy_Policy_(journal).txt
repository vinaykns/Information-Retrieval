<p><i><b>Energy Policy</b></i> is a monthly <a href="/wiki/Peer-reviewed" class="mw-redirect" title="Peer-reviewed">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Academic_journal" title="Academic journal">academic journal</a> covering research on <a href="/wiki/Energy_policy" title="Energy policy">energy policy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Energy_supply" title="Energy supply">energy supply</a>. It is published by <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a>. According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2013 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 2.696.<sup id="cite_ref-WoS_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WoS-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
