units Pacific Power is a regulated electric utility with service territory throughout Oregon northern California and southeastern Washington Rocky Mountain Power is a regulated electric utility with service territory throughout Utah Wyoming and southeastern Idaho PacifiCorp operates one of the largest privately held transmission systems in the US within the western Energy Imbalance Market In 2001
