 Autosport International is a fourday event motorshow that includes a two day dedicated trade show for industry professionals and a two day dedicated public show for motorsport fans It is held at the NEC in Birmingham United Kingdom and operated by Autosport Media UK part of Motorsport Network usually in the second week of January The 2018 show will be held on 11th 14th January Its vital statistics include 27000 trade visitors and 60000 public visitors in attendance with over 5000 overseas visitors and an 80 male demographic All categories of track and offroad motor racing are represented including Formula One BTCC 24 Hours of Le Mans and much much more The show also has an adrenaline packed Live Action Arena where spectators can witness motorsport action and stunts up close and personal The show has close links with many industry magazine titles These publications inlclude Autosport Motorsport News and F1 Racing who all actively participate at the show Industry bodies also have close affiliations entitling members to many discounts and offers These include the Motor Sports Association and the British Racing and Sports Car Club References External links Autosport International exhibitions website autosportcom
